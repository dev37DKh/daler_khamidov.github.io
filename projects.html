Projects

I have been involved in various projects that combine advanced modeling with practical applications. Some key projects include:

1. MI-NMPC of heating system

Model Predictive Control of a Heat Pump System with PVT Collectors and Ice Storage

This project showcases our innovative approach to applying MI-NMPC for an integrated heating system. The poster presentation at eurosun2024 highlighted our work, and the full paper is expected to be published soon at https://www.eurosun2024.org/.

2. Data Analysis

Data Analysis of Germany’s Electricity Imports and Exports

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of Germany’s electricity import and export data. Advanced statistical methods and visualization techniques were employed to identify trends, forecast market behavior, and provide valuable insights into the dynamics of the energy market.

3. Forecasting of key indicators

Forecasting Key Indicators for Gas Condensate Field Development

This project focused on forecasting key indicators for the development of gas condensate fields. Using advanced predictive analytics and modeling techniques, the project provides strategic insights aimed at supporting future field development and optimization efforts.
